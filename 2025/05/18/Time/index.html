Time

Posted on 2025-05-18 In Read

费尔南多·佩索阿对时间的思考挺有趣的：时钟是从外部对时间的一个空间化分割，心理时间的主观感受只是分割对时间的感觉。
Fernando Pessoa’s thinking about time is quite interesting: the clock is a spatial division of time from the outside, and the subjective feeling of time is just a division of the feeling of time.

日落日出，人们将世界分成一天、一月、一年，给人赋以年龄，将人生岁月分割成幼、少、青、中、老，各自赋予每个阶段以意义和价值，走过此段即为失去，行色匆匆地，似乎在不停歇地跨越障碍。被时间追赶着，始终后悔着上一段没有走好的岁月。时间若被划为碎片，我感到人生的残缺。
Sunset and sunrise, people divide the world into one day, one month, one year, give people age, divide life into childhood, youth, youth, middle age, old age, and give each stage a meaning and value. Each stage is a barrier to be crossed, and we always regret the past. If time is divided into fragments, I feel the incomplete life of human beings.

时间是虚幻的，而岁月是场骗局。我们在噩梦中做着美梦。
Time is illusionary, and years are a fraud. We dream of beauty in our nightmares.
